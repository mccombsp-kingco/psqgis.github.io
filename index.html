This is a group for folks interested in QGIS, the free, open-source Geographic Information System software. We are located in the Puget Sound Region of Washington State.

All skill levels are welcome! We meet monthly to share topics of interest. Usually on the second Tuesday of the month. Bring your questions, or share examples of what you've been able to accomplish with the rest of the group. The idea is to learn more about this very capable mapping and analysis package.

Check out our blog for meeting announcements, notes from previous meetings, and other items of interest.

Currently our meetup.com group is maxed for our subscription level at 50 people. We are in the process of migrating to Google Groups to accommodate more members.
